About Me

My name is Alexander Gotfrid,

I am attending Business Information Technology Program at Red River College and working towards a title of Full Stack Web Developerer.


I am a continuous learner who always strives to improve and grow as an individual and my goal is to become a Full Stack web developer. In addition to my college courses, I am regularly taking courses at Udemy and Linda, and currently working on my skills in React, Python and Ruby. My passion for programming and project development led me to learn a variety of programming languages and technologies. In my spare time I enjoy building applications and websites.


I am the owner of Alexander Gotfrid Enterprise (A.G.E), which focuses on software and web development, network design and low voltage cable installations.


In the past I have been managing a painting franchise with College Pro. Immediately before becoming a franchise owner, I have worked as a professional painter before taking over the College Pro. My team and I have completed over 100 exterior and interior residential projects in the Winnipeg and Steinbach area. Every single year our focus is to provide exceptional customer service and quality of work. The pride we take in our work and our customer-centric approach has allowed myself and my team to enjoy high degrees of satisfaction from many clients.


Operating a robust painting business has been a tremendously fulfilling experience; it channeled my passion towards entrepreneurship and allowed me to learn and developed leadership, public speaking, team building and management skills.


I am currently seeking new opportunities as a Software Developer.
