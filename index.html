About Me

My name is Alexander Gotfrid, and I am the founder and owner of Alexander Gotfrid Enterprise (A.G.E). The goal of my company is to provide my customers with the best possible experience. I hope to earn your business through great communication, hard work, exceptional leadership and a high level of customer service.


A.G.E focuses on software and web development, network design and low voltage cable installations as well as commercial and residential painting.


In the past I have been running painting franchise with College Pro Painters. Immediately before becoming a franchise owner, I have worked as a professional painter before taking over the College Pro. My team and I have completed over 100 exterior and interior residential projects in the Winnipeg and Steinbach area. Every single year our focus is to provide exceptional customer service and quality of work. The pride we take in our work and our customer-centric approach has allowed myself and my team to enjoy high degrees of satisfaction from many clients.


Operating a robust painting business has been a tremendously fulfilling experience; it channeled my passion and allowed me to find a deep interest in the home improvement business.


I have graduated from University of Manitoba with a Bachelor of Science in Microbiology. Presently and I am working towards Business Information Technology Diploma at Red River College. My intention is to earn your business by working together with you and to gain a clear understanding of your needs and expectations to provide you with exceptional service.


You can expect to receive a value packed, quality project at a competitive price on the market. My promise to you is to work harder to win, respect, and sustain your trust more than any other company.


I sincerely look forward to the opportunity to earn your business, and provide you with excellent service to build a long term business relationship!
